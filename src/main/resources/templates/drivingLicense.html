Driving License

01
Exchange of a foreign driving license with a similar Romanian document

In order to change the driving license, the applicant shall personally submit to the community public service, the following documents:

    1. The national driving license issued by the authorities of the states provided in article (2) paragraph (1) of the Annex (1) whose change is requested, in original. If the driving license has been issued by the authorities of the states provided in article (2) paragraph (1) letters b) and c) of the Annexes (2) and (3) is also submitted the legalized translation into Romanian; or
    2. The document certifying the loss, theft or unjustified absence of the driving license, if the latter has been issued by the authorities of the EU Member States. If the document is not written in Romanian, a legalized translation into Romanian is also submitted.
  2. The identity document of the applicant, as well as the documents proving the domicile or residence in Romania provided in article (1) paragraph (6) of the order
  3. The request of the applicant
  4. If a driving license with a new administrative validity is requested, the file for changing the driving license must also contain the document issued by an authorized healthcare unit showing that the holder is fit to drive motor vehicles from the categories mentioned in the driving license submitted for change
  5. Proof of payment of the fee provided by law - 89 lei

02
Issue of a Romanian driving license with a new administrative validity

  1. The request of the applicant which is obtained and completed in electronic format at the booking office, together with the theft/loss declaration, as the case may be
  2. Identity card of the applicant, in original and in copy
  3. Proof of payment of the equivalent value for the driving license - 89 lei
  4. The original driving license
  5. The document issued by an authorized medical unit which shows that the holder is medially fit to drive vehicles of the category for which he requests the issuance of the driving license(medical file).

03
Issue of a duplicate of the Romanian driving license

  1. The request of the applicant
  2. Identity card of the applicant, in original and in copy
  3. Proof of payment of the equivalent value for the driving license - 89 lei
  4. The original driving license, in case of damage

    5. The duplicate shall be issued for the period of validity of the exchanged document.

    In order to issue a driving license with a new administrative validity, it is necessary to present the document issued by an authorized medical unit, which shows that the holder is medially fit to drive vehicles in the category for which the driving license is requested(medical file)

04
Issue of a driving license in the case of Romanian citizens who have their domicile or residence in Romania and are temporarily abroad

In the case of Romanian citizens temporarily abroad but who have their domicile or residence in Romania, the application for the issuance of the driving license may be submitted by proxy, authorized by special power of attorney authenticated by the diplomatic missions or consular offices of Romania.

The power of attorney may also be authenticated by foreign authorities, in which case they must meet the conditions of over-legalization provided by law or have the signature applied according to the Hague Convention.

  1. The request of the applicant
  2. Two recent color photographs of the holder, in the format 35 x 38.5 mm, which are applied on request, in the designated space, respectively in the upper right corner of the special power of attorney and are certified by applying the dry stamp of the diplomatic mission / consular post
  3. Proof of payment of the equivalent value of the driving license - 89 lei
  4. The driving license, when it is in the possession of the holder - in case of loss or theft of the driving license the holder declares this fact by completing the application accordingly

    5. In the case of Romanian citizens, a copy of the identity card of the holder, from which it results that he has his domicile or residence in Romania

    In the case of foreign citizens, a copy of the residence card, residence permit, registration certificate accompanied by a copy of the passport, diplomatic passport / service

  5. The document issued by an authorized medical unit, from which it results that the holder is medically fit to drive vehicles of the category for which he requests the issuance of the driving license
  6. The special power of attorney and the identity document of the applicant, in original and copy
