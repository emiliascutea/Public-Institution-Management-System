Passports

01
Simple Electronic Passport

  1. Identity card, provisional identity card or, as the case may be, valid identity card, in original. The temporary identity card must be accompanied by the original birth certificate.
  2. The document proving the payment of the passport, in original, or the payment document published on paper containing all the information related to the payment made by means of online payment, completed in the name of the holder.
  3. Previous passport, if any.

02
Simple Temporary Passport

  1. Identity card, provisional identity card or, as the case may be, valid identity card, in original. The temporary identity card must be accompanied by the original birth certificate.
  2. The document proving the payment of the passport, in original, or the payment document published on paper containing all the information related to the payment made by means of online payment, completed in the name of the holder.
  3. Previous passport, if any.

03
Deadline for Issuing a Simple Electronic Passport

Applications shall be dealt with by the competent authorities within time limits from the date of their submission and may not exceed:
  • 15 working days, in case of applications for the issuance of simple electronic passports submitted in the country
  • 45 working days, in case of applications regarding the issuance of simple electronic passports submitted abroad, at the diplomatic missions and consular offices of Romania in the member states of the European Union.
  • 60 working days, in case of applications regarding the issuance of simple electronic passports submitted abroad, at the diplomatic missions and consular offices of Romania from third countries in relation to the European Union

If the applicant submits the application for the issuance of the simple electronic passport abroad, but opts for its withdrawal from one of the Community public passport services, the deadline for issuance is 15 working days.

If the applicant submits the application for the issuance of the simple electronic passport in the country, but opts for its withdrawal from the diplomatic missions or consular offices of Romania abroad, the deadline for issuance is, if applicable, 45 working days (for collection from Member States of the European Union) or 60 working days (for withdrawal from third countries in relation to the European Union).

04
Deadline for Issuing a Simple Temporary Passport

A person who declares that he does not wish to be issued a simple electronic passport will be issued, on request, a temporary simple passport within 15 working days.

The requests shall be resolved by the competent authorities, in terms that run from the date of their submission and which may not exceed 3 working days, in the following situations:
  • If the holder declares in writing that he is going to travel to countries between which there is a state of belligerence or diplomatic conflict and has a stamp or visa issued in his simple passport or electronic passport issued by the state with which the country in which he travels is in conflict.
  • When the holder holds a simple passport or simple electronic passport containing valid visas, but as a result of the exhaustion of the files intended for their application, travel to that state or to states other than those issuing visas is no longer possible.
  • When the holder has submitted the simple passport or the simple electronic passport for obtaining visas and declares that he has to travel abroad as a matter of urgency.
  • When the minor is going to travel abroad for studies or to participate in official competitions or to follow a medical treatment without which his life or health is endangered and there is no time needed to obtain the consent of the other parent.
  • For persons temporarily deprived of the capacity to exercise, as well as for persons who, for medical reasons, are unable to express their will or to appear before the competent authority.
  • In objective situations where, for health, family or professional reasons, the presence of the person on the territory of another state is necessary and urgent and there is no time required for the issuance of the simple electronic passport.

05
Expiration dates

The simple electronic passport is valid:
  • 3 years for minors under 12 years of age.
  • 5 years for people between 12 and 18 years old.
  • 10 years for people over 18 years.

The temporary simple passport is valid for 12 months.

The validity of passports shall cease by right on the date on which the competent authorities find that they have been damaged or destroyed or, as the case may be, the existence in their contents of any erasures or alterations made without right.

Simple passports, issued before the entry into force of the electronic simple passport, remain valid until the date entered in the computerized tab.
