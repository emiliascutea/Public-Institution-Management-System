Registration Number

01
Keeping the registration number combination

Keeping the registration number combination may be requested by the holder of the registration certificate, until the moment of deregistration of the vehicle, expiration of the previous application for keeping or transcripting of the transfer of ownership in the name of the acquirer, as the case may be.

The request of signing up in the records of the responsive authorities is made based on the submission of the following documents:
  1. the request of the applicant
  2. the identity document of the applicant or the documents which certify acquisition of legal personality, name and headquarters of the legal person, in original and copy
  3. the proof of payment of the charge for keeping the registration number combination - 30 lei

02
Deregistration of a vehicle in circulation from Romania (export)

  1. The request of the applicant
  2. Registration certificate - original
  3. Envelopes with registration number
  4. Vehicle identity card - original
  5. The document which certifies the final removal of the vehicle from Romania
  6. Fiscal attestation certificate
  7. Certificate of deregistration - 3 copies
  8. Identity card of the applicant, in original and in copy
  9. In the case of legal persons, the documents attesting the acquisition of legal personality, the name and the headquarters, in copy
  10. Special power of attorney authenticated by the notary, in case the documents are submitted by a person other than the holder
If the registration certificate, the vehicle's identity card or the registration plates have been withheld by the foreign authorities upon registration in another state, the applicant shall provide proof of their detention.

03
Issue of a temporary movement permit

  1. The request of the applicant
  2. Copy of the contract of compulsory motor third party liability insurance for damages caused by third parties by vehicle and tram accidents, concluded in the name of the owner of the vehicle, within its validity term
  3. The document certifying the applicant's ownership of the vehicle, in original and copy
  4. Registration documents issued by foreign authorities in the case of a vehicle which has been registered in another state, in original and copy

    5. In the absence of these documents, the provisional authorization for traffic will be made for a maximum period of 5 days, but which may not exceed the date on which the compulsory motor third party liability insurance expires.

  5. The document which certifies the final removal of the vehicle from Romania

    6. In the case of legal entities, the documents certifying the acquisition of legal personality, name and registered office, in copy

  6. Identity card of the applicant, in original and copy
  7. Proof of payment of the equivalent value of the temporary circulation authorization - 13 lei
  8. A copy of the customs declaration, as the case may be, for vehicles purchased from outside the EU, for which the provisional movement authorization is requested for a period of more than 30 days or for periods which, in total, exceed 30 days
  9. Special power of attorney authenticated by the notary public, in case the documents are submitted by a person other than the holder.

04
Issue of a license plate(s) number claimed to be lost, stolen or damaged

  1. The request of the applicant
  2. Declaration of loss/theft, as the case may be
  3. The damaged license plate(s), as the case may be
  4. Proof of payment of the equivalent value of the new license plates : 20 lei - plate type A(rectangular) or 40 lei - pair, 23 lei - plate type B(square) or 17 lei - plate type C(moto)
  5. Identity card of the applicant, original and copy
  6. In the case of legal entities, the documents attesting the acquisition of legal personality, the name and the headquarters, in copy
  7. Special power of attorney authenticated by the notary, in case the documents are submitted by a person other than the holder.
