Taxes

01
License plate fees, preferential number assignment, number retention

Payment is made by internet banking, bank transfer, money order in the account of the state treasury or cash at the booking office once the documents are submitted.
  • 20 lei / plate type A - normal plates with the size 110 x 520mm, used for all vehicles (2 plates - 40 lei)
  • 23 lei / plate type B - rectangular plates with a size of 200x340mm (also described as ?squares?), used only for the rear of trailers or 4 x 4 vehicles, if they have such a space provided in the construction
  • 17 lei / plate type C - plates used only for motorcycles, size 130x240 mm
  • 45 lei / operation - assignment of preferential number: assignment of a combination of the registration number, at the choice of the solitaire to which is added the value of the plates with registration number (type A, B or C)
  • 30 lei / operation - keeping the number: keeping the combination of the registration number before the alienation of the vehicle

02
Fees for driving licenses, registration certificates and temporary driving license

The equivalent value of the driving license - 89 lei, of the registration certificate - 49 lei, of the provisional circulation authorization - 13 lei, is collected in the account of BCR, CEC BANK and the network of SelfPay Payment Stations

The payment terminals are located in the BCR, CEC Bank units and in the network of SelfPay Payment Stations located in supermarkets, malls, convenience stores, universities

The following means of payment are used to pay the fees: bank transfer, online means of payment, National Electronic Online Payment System(SNEP), POS installed by at the issuing public service offices, postal order, other means of payment, provided by law

We remind you that the payment of these taxes can no longer be paid in cash at the booking offices of the community public services for driving licenses and vehicle registration, once the documents have been submitted!

03
Fees for passports

For simple electronic passport:
  • 258 lei - for persons who have reached the age of 12.
  • 234 lei - for persons under 12 years of age.

For temporary simple passport:
  • 96 lei - regardless of age.
