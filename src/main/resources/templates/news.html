December 23

Info

Local taxes and fees can be paid until the end of the year, as usual, both at the cash registers of the Fiscal Directorate from all City Hall offices and at the post offices. Only on Friday, December 31, the program will be shorter, until 09.00 and it will be possible to make payments only at the cashiers at the headquarters of the City Hall. The reduction of the program is necessary because the money collected must be deposited at the Cluj-Napoca Treasury until 10.00.
